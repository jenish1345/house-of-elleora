📸 HOW TO ADD YOUR LOGO:

1. Save the logo image you shared (the pink circular one with "House of Elleora")

2. Rename it to: logo.png

3. Copy it to the "public" folder in this project

4. Replace the existing "logo.png" file

5. Refresh the website - your logo will appear!

The logo will show in:
- Top left corner of the website (navigation bar)
- Next to "House of Elleora" text

Recommended logo size: 200x200 pixels or larger (square format works best)

That's it! 🎉
